FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

What format should I send my footage in?

Any flat or log profile works great: S-Log2, S-Log3, V-Log, C-Log, Log C, BRAW, R3D, or ProRes RAW. The flatter the source, the more latitude I have to work with. Standard Rec.709 footage is also accepted — just note it limits the grade range. I'll confirm the ideal format in your intake form after booking.

How do I transfer large video files?

After payment, you'll receive SwissTransfer upload instructions via confirmation email. SwissTransfer handles up to 50 GB per transfer with no account required — just upload and share the link. It's free, fast, and built for large media files.

What software do you use?

DaVinci Resolve Studio, with full node-based manual grading. Every shot is graded by hand — no automated tools, no batch LUT applications, no shortcuts. I typically work in ACES or a custom CST pipeline depending on your camera profile and deliverable requirements.

Can I request a specific look or visual reference?

Absolutely — references are strongly encouraged. Film stills, Instagram screenshots, mood boards, other colorists' work, anything works. The more specific your direction in the intake form, the closer the first pass will land and the fewer revision rounds you'll need.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

Every package includes revision rounds (Starter: 2, Pro: 3, Studio: unlimited). I'll rework the grade until it reflects your vision. If after exhausting all revision rounds the work doesn't meet expectations, I'll work with you directly on a resolution. I stand behind every project I deliver.

Do you offer rush delivery?

Rush delivery (48-hour turnaround) is available for Starter and Pro tiers at an additional flat fee of $75. Studio rush orders are evaluated case-by-case depending on project scope. Email before booking to confirm availability for time-sensitive work.
